## Business Problem
Understand post-conversion customer behavior and revenue performance by analyzing:
- Time to purchase
- Device and channel differences
- Geographic revenue performance
- Behavioral signals impacting revenue

This project simulates work done by Product and Growth Analysts supporting product optimization and revenue strategy.

## Key KPIs Analyzed
- Unique Customers: 4,419  
- Total Revenue: $362.2K  
- Purchases: 5,691  
- Average Time to Purchase: 79 minutes  

---

## Analytical Focus Areas

### 1. Revenue vs Time-to-Purchase
- Weekly analysis comparing revenue trends against average time to purchase.
- Identified periods where longer purchase times correlated with reduced revenue efficiency.

### 2. Device Performance
- Desktop generates highest revenue volume.
- Mobile shows strong revenue but longer time-to-purchase.
- Tablet lowest revenue, suggesting limited optimization priority.

### 3. Channel (Medium) Insights
- Organic and Referral channels generate strongest revenue.
- CPC shows faster purchase times but weaker total revenue, suggesting potential for optimization.

### 4. Geographic Performance
- United States contributes highest revenue overall.
- International markets (UK, India, Canada) show strong engagement with potential expansion opportunity.

---

## Business Recommendations
- Optimize mobile purchase experience to reduce time-to-purchase friction.
- Double down on Organic and Referral acquisition channels.
- Run experiments on checkout flow during weeks with high time-to-purchase variance.
- Consider regional growth strategies for high-performing international markets.
